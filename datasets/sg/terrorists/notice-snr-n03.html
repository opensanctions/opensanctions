Financial Measures in Relation to Violent Israeli Settlers

Issued pursuant to:Section 3(1) of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2022

Singapore has imposed targeted sanctions on four Israeli individuals for their involvement in acts of violence against Palestinians in the West Bank. These include financial measures which apply to all financial institutions in Singapore.

Details can be found in the Notice below, which applies with immediate effect.
